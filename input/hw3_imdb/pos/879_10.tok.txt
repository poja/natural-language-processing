an excellent thriller of all time .
vijay anand excels as director and editor .
performances by dev anand , vyjayantimala , tanuja and ashok kumar prove to be an asset to the film which has its screenplay worked out to the minutest detail .
cinematography adds to the mystery , glamour and other requirements of the story as it evolves .
and to top it all , music by sachin dev burman is unforgettable .
`` yeh dil na hota bechara '' by kishore kumar , `` raat akeli hai '' by asha bhosle , `` rula ke gaya sapna mera '' by lata , `` aasman ke niche '' by lata and rafi and `` hothon pe aisi baat main '' by lata , again reaffirm burman dada 's unique stature as a top notch composer with a style of his own .
